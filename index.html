Aksharsparsh Matimand Children School

Aksharsparsh Matimand Children School run by the Sewa Trust, is dedicated to supporting children with intellectual disabilities. The school provides specialized education, therapies, and life skills training, aiming to enhance these children's quality of life and integrate them into society. Through compassionate care and community involvement, Aksharsparsh empowers children with special needs to achieve greater independence and confidence.
